### How It Works

2. Choose WAV settings if needed.
3. Convert and download the WAV file.

### Why Convert MP3 to WAV

解释应克制，避免暗示有损音频转 WAV 会恢复丢失质量。推荐表述：

MP3 to WAV conversion is useful when an editor, DAW, transcription tool, or broadcast workflow requires WAV input. It does not restore audio detail already removed by MP3 compression.
