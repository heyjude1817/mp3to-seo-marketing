### Why Convert WAV to MP3

核心解释：

WAV files are often large because they usually store uncompressed audio. MP3 reduces file size by using lossy compression, making files easier to share, upload, and stream.

### Recommended Settings

| Scenario | Suggested Bitrate |
| --- | --- |

| Podcasts | 128 kbps |
| Music sharing | 192 kbps |
| Higher quality music | 256-320 kbps |
